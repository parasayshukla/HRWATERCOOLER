Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our water coolers

What capacity water cooler should I buy for my office?

+

For 10-25 people: 20L, For 25-50 people: 30-50L, For 50-100 people: 100L, For 100+ people: 150L or 500L. Consider peak usage hours and whether it's for drinking only or includes other uses.

Do you provide installation services?

+

Yes! We provide FREE installation service across Gujarat and pan-India. Our trained technicians will install the cooler, demonstrate its usage, and ensure everything works perfectly.

What is the warranty period?

+

We provide 2 years warranty on 20L-150L models and 3 years warranty on 500L industrial models. Extended warranty options are available. Warranty covers manufacturing defects and compressor issues.

How much electricity does a water cooler consume?

+

Our energy-efficient models consume 40% less power than conventional coolers. A 50L cooler typically uses 3-4 units per day, costing approximately ₹50-70 per month in electricity.

What makes HR Water Cooler different from others?

+

We use food-grade SS 304/316 stainless steel, provide free installation, offer 2-3 year warranty, have 24/7 customer support, and proudly manufacture in India. Our coolers are ISO certified and built to last 10+ years.

Can I get a customized water cooler?

+

Yes! We offer customization for industrial models (150L, 500L). You can customize capacity, number of taps, mounting options, color, and additional features. Contact us with your requirements.

How do I maintain my water cooler?

+

Simple maintenance: Clean the tank monthly, replace filters every 3-6 months, check for leaks, ensure proper ventilation around compressor. Our team provides free maintenance guidance and video tutorials.

Do you ship outside Gujarat?

+

Yes, we ship pan-India! Delivery time: 3-5 days within Gujarat, 7-10 days for other states. We also provide international shipping for bulk orders. Installation support available across India.
